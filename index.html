Schedule of events
June 9 – 11 · Paris
Paris, Le Marais
We kick things off in the city of light. Cobblestone streets, the Seine, the Pompidou, perfect croissants. Explore at your own pace — we'll share our favorite spots closer to the date.
June 12 · Friday
Arrive in Guéthary
Check in to Villa Anvers perched above Parlementia. Settle in, breathe the Atlantic air, then dinner together in Guéthary at 7pm. Restaurant: Heteroclito.
June 13 · Saturday · The Big Day
The Wedding
  • Morning on the beach
  • Breakfast together
  • Ceremony on the bluff at 5:00pm
  • Drinks & photos in the courtyard at 6:00pm
  • Dinner at Villa Anvers at 7:00pm
June 14 · Sunday
Morning surf, day in town, then afternoon and dinner in Biarritz
Morning surf session, then a beach day followed by a trip to Biarritz for drinks and dinner at Bar Jean or Cafe Du Commerce.
June 15 · Monday
Afternoon in Biarritz
Wander the Grande Plage, explore the markets, grab a coffee on the cliffs. The original surf town of Europe, just 20 min away. Return surfboards.
June 16 – 20 · French Riviera
South of France
Onward to Juan-les-Pins on the Côte d'Azur. Mediterranean gardens, turquoise water, and the charming streets of Antibes just steps away.
Getting there
For Paris
CDG or ORY
Both major Paris airports are well-connected globally. Le Marais is ~45 min from CDG via RER B + Metro.
For Guéthary
Biarritz Airport (BIQ)
~15 min drive to Villa Anvers. Direct flights from Paris ~1.5 hrs. Or TGV Paris → Bayonne (~4.5 hrs).
For French Riviera
Nice Airport (NCE)
~25 min drive to Juan-les-Pins. Direct flights from Biarritz or Paris. Train from Nice to Antibes ~30 min.
